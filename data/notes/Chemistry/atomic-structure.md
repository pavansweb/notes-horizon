# Atomic Structure & Quantum Mechanics

## 1. Bohr's Atomic Model (Extended)
### 1.1 Postulates & Equations
- **Angular Momentum:** $mvr = n\frac{h}{2\pi}$.
- **Radius ($r_n$):** $0.529 \frac{n^2}{Z} \text{ \AA}$.
- **Velocity ($v_n$):** $2.18 \times 10^6 \frac{Z}{n} \text{ m/s}$.
- **Energy ($E_n$):** $-13.6 \frac{Z^2}{n^2} \text{ eV/atom}$.
- **Relationship:** $TE = -KE = \frac{1}{2} PE$.

### 1.2 Hydrogen Spectrum
- **Rydberg Formula:** $1/\lambda = R Z^2 (1/n_1^2 - 1/n_2^2)$.
- **Number of lines:** If an electron jumps from $n_2$ to $n_1$, total lines $= \frac{(n_2-n_1)(n_2-n_1+1)}{2}$.

## 2. Quantum Mechanical Model
### 2.1 Wave Nature and Uncertainty
- **de-Broglie:** $\lambda = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2mqV}} = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2mK}}$.
- **Heisenberg:** $\Delta x \cdot \Delta p \ge \frac{h}{4\pi}$. Applicable to microscopic particles only.

### 2.2 Quantum Numbers and Orbitals
- **Principal ($n$):** Size and energy. $1, 2, 3, \dots$
- **Azimuthal ($l$):** Shape. $0$ to $(n-1)$. $s=0, p=1, d=2, f=3$.
- **Magnetic ($m_l$):** Orientation. $-l$ to $+l$.
- **Spin ($m_s$):** $\pm 1/2$.
- **Nodes:** Total $= n-1$. Radial $= n-l-1$. Angular $= l$.

## 3. Electronic Principles
- **Aufbau Principle:** Fill in order of increasing $(n+l)$. If $(n+l)$ same, lower $n$ fills first.
- **Pauli Exclusion:** No two electrons have the same 4 QNs. 
- **Hund's Rule:** Maximize total spin by filling degenerate orbitals singly first.

## Typed Problems (JEE Main PYQ Based)

### Type 1: Bohr Model (Frequency and Energy)
**Q1.** Find the ratio of frequency of revolution of electron in 2nd orbit of $He^+$ to 3rd orbit of $Li^{2+}$.
**Concept:** $f = v/2\pi r \propto (Z/n) / (n^2/Z) = Z^2/n^3$.
**Solution:**
1. $f_1 / f_2 = (Z_1^2 / n_1^3) / (Z_2^2 / n_2^3) = (2^2 / 2^3) / (3^2 / 3^3) = (4/8) / (9/27)$.
2. $= (1/2) / (1/3) = 3/2$.

### Type 2: Uncertainty Principle
**Q1.** If uncertainty in position and momentum are equal, find the uncertainty in velocity.
**Concept:** $\Delta x = \Delta p$. Use $\Delta x \cdot \Delta p = h/4\pi$.
**Solution:**
1. $(\Delta p)^2 = h/4\pi \Rightarrow \Delta p = \sqrt{h/4\pi}$.
2. $m \Delta v = \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{h/\pi} \Rightarrow \Delta v = \frac{1}{2m} \sqrt{h/\pi}$.

### Type 3: Radial Nodes
**Q1.** Find the number of radial and angular nodes in a $4d$ orbital.
**Concept:** Radial $= n-l-1$, Angular $= l$.
**Solution:**
1. For $4d$: $n=4, l=2$.
2. Radial $= 4 - 2 - 1 = 1$.
3. Angular $= 2$.
